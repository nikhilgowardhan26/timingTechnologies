Timing India is a tech company established in 2009, powered by individuals who are trained and primed in modern technological practices, exceeding our knowledge with time, and working to heighten the client's expectations.

It all started with our immense inspiration for the RFID Technology, how convenient and hands-free it is to access, making it accurate and automated, successively saving lots of time and energy. We are the industry-leading timing services provider, trusted by businesses worldwide, be they small or large. The products and solutions designed by us are fit-for-purpose, that streamline processes - all within a controlled, secure, and audit-ready cloud platform.

It’s not what we do, it’s all why we do.

Mission

Consistency and Sustainability are believed from day one by the team and the bottom line is to grow with utter conscience and succeed.

We are here to inspire, innovate and impact the world through modern technology.

Vision

To keep delivering our technological services, outlooking satisfaction and content of our customers, crossing one milestone at a time.

A will to win in this competitive world

The world being a battleground for every business has simultaneously inflated choices for the consumers. But their trust remains in the one who conclusively delivers the best quality at a reasonable price. We at Timing, enhance our domain knowledge timely, to produce better technological solutions while being economically rational.

We believe in one thing at a time

A variety of solutions have been implemented from time to time. There always exists a dilemma on what works best. We tend to keep our balance on utilizing and implementing the technological solutions we firmly believe can deliver the best results. We trust in delivering with utmost quality solving one problem at a time.

A constant need to do better than great

Our approach to outlooking the transformational adaptations in every client that puts their trust in us is unique. It doesn't necessarily mean we are perfect. We do make mistakes, learn through them, and come back stronger. Each solution we provide has a different standpoint which strongly gives the top-notch attributes that suit a problem in every possible way.
